Yehya Altameimi

Chief Executive Officer

Yehya Altameimi was appointed Chief Executive Officer of Saudi Aramco Nabors Drilling, or SANAD upon its inception in December 2017. Previously, he served as Vice President of Nabors' International Drilling and Rig Services business and the General Manager of its Saudi Arabia operations. Prior to joining Nabors in 2013, he held operational, technical, and country manager positions of increasing responsibility for several large oilfield services companies since 1994. Yehya graduated from Watterson College with a Diploma in Electronics in 1994 and from the Missouri University of Science and Technology in 1997 with a BS in Petroleum Engineering.
